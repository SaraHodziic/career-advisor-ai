Key Findings:
- The resume classification model achieved an accuracy of 65.39%.
- The recommendation system performed best for technology-related resumes.
- Skill-based matching successfully identified overlapping skills between resumes and job descriptions.
- Recommendation quality decreased for categories that were underrepresented in the job dataset.
- The mismatch between the resume dataset (24 categories) and the job dataset (primarily technical roles) was the main limitation affecting recommendation quality.

Conclusion:
The system successfully classified resumes, identified matching and missing skills, and generated job recommendations. Results were strongest for technical resumes and demonstrated the importance of dataset alignment when developing AI-powered recommendation systems.
